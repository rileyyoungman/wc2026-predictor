🏑 How to Play
Step 1 — Predict the Group Stage

Head to the Group Stage tab and enter a score for every match. You need to fill in all 72 games before you can fill out the knockout bracket — this is required to correctly seed the 3rd place teams.

Step 2 — Fill Out the Knockout Bracket

Head to the Knockout tab. Click any match to predict the score and pick a winner. Winners automatically advance to the next round. If you predict a draw, you'll be asked to enter a penalty shootout score to decide the winner.

Step 3 — Watch the Points Roll In

Results update automatically after each game. Check the Leaderboard tab to see how you stack up. If the site has been updated recently you may need to hard-refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R on PC, Cmd+Shift+R on Mac) to see the latest version.
